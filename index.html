Pitch Map

Field zones · Kick strategy · Phase triggers · Decision framework

Field Zones
Kick Strategy by Zone
🟡 Gold Zone

Ruthless mentality. Strike first 3 phases. Must come away with points. 3 pod carries max — then attack space or kick to score.

🟢 Green Zone

Build pressure. Identify space with multi-option attack. Keep ball moving. Queen options. Only kick to score.

🟡 Amber Zone

Relieve through territory first. Run/Kick/Pass from set piece. 3 phases then kick if no dominance. Apply pressure, retrieve.

🔴 Red Zone

Get out fast. Minimise rucks. Kick long and contest the lineout. Do not give the opposition easy opportunities.

Defence System

Line defence · Blitz · Fold system · Transition · Call language

Defence is an attitude. Great defence is a way of life. Mentality → Behaviours → Effort. Every phase, every tackle.
🔄 Defence Cycle
🧠 Smart · Fast · Aggressive · Accurate
S
Smart: Scan early, see threats, communicate early. Set fast on outside shoulder with equal spacings. Think ahead of the game.
F
Fast: Load Up then Go. Fast for 5m square. Hunt hard from outside in. Speed is our weapon in defence.
A
Aggressive: Win the ruck. Hunt hard, win the jackal race. WIND = attack the space. MIST = screen and cover.
A
Accurate: Win the collision. Good tackle selection — 2 in the tackle. Select well, be confident, commit.
🛡️ Line Defence Options
STORM — Blitz Line
Aggressive rush defence. Load Up → Go hard together. 5m square, hunt from outside. Used on opposition half first phase.
TIDE — Hold Line
Contain and control. Stay connected, hold width, don't rush. Used when defending own 22 or against wide threats.
FROST — Fold System (CBA)
C (1st) → pushes wide, 1st threat. B (2nd) → mid-channel, 1st receiver. A (3rd) → closest to ruck, focus on 9.
Weather calls: STORM · TIDE · FROST · WIND · MIST
⚡ Transition — Attack to Defence
SPEED TO SET
React instantly when possession is lost. Zero time wasted.
APPLY PRESSURE
Get the line up. STORM within the first 20 seconds of turnover.
REGAIN CONTROL
Contest at breakdown. Slow ball. Win the ruck or force a penalty.

Phase Plays

Attack shape · Pod structure · Phase triggers · Decision framework

1·3·3·1 Shape. Organisation and clarity. Maximise space, generate mismatches, keep the ball always moving (BLUE / WHITE).
⚡ 1·3·3·1 Attack Shape
2 Forwards on Edge · Forward Pods North/South · Connection is Key
🔵 BLUE — Ball off 9

Played off 9 with attached player (GOLD option). First player in pod sets on outside shoulder of defender 3. Inside tip limited. Outside tip when D3/D4 bites.

⚪ WHITE — Ball off 10

Played off 10 with depth. 10 to 1 who tips to 2 or 3 if space opens. 10 straight to GOLD if defence blitzing. Use 22-to-22 to get ball to edge.

🟡 GOLD — Queen Option

Attached player behind pod leader. Play when defence is narrow and passive. Call STORM to demand the ball. Non-negotiable: set on outside shoulder of defender.

Restart Principles

Kick-off attack · Reception · Forward responsibilities

⚡
Aggression
Good aggression and linespeed. Contest and chase hard on every kick-off. No passengers.
🎯
Compete
Left/right wing or back row compete if kick is contestable. Urgency off the ball to support catcher is key.
🧠
Decide Early
Full lift or no lift — the call is made before the ball arrives. Decision before landing.
📋 Forward Restart Principles
1
Never allow a straight chase line. Get a body in front of the chaser before the ball arrives. Shield the catcher at all costs.
2
Urgency off the ball. Every player moves to their restart role before the ball is kicked. No reacting — anticipating.
3
Gather loose ball immediately. Players around the catcher must be ready to secure any loose ball — react, don't watch.
4
Apply pressure on exit kicks. Make their kicker rush every decision. Contest every opposition exit.
